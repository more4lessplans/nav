"What is the coverage of your plans like?"

Our Bottomless Data Plan gives you service in Canada, USA, and Mexico. We do not have any infrustructure of our own. We operate entirely off major carrier networks. The network and speeds are all top tier with full LTE service. Check the appropriate websites for speeds and coverage maps.

"I've seen people charging $600 or more to setup an unlimited data plan" 

There is no real competition, since this is the only offer of it's kind. We beleive in competitive, fair pricing. We provide this service to help Canadians save money, while enjoying worry free benifits.

"I'm already with a differet provider, am I still eligible?""

Yes. This plan is available to all who bring their own device!

"Can I keep my number?"

The short answer is yes! But you will not be able to port your number into this plan. Our bottomless data plan comes with a USA number. Although it's still possible to keep your canadian number if you have a dual sim phone or if you use an app!

"Can I get a new phone?""

We reccomend to everyone to bring their own device. This is actually the cheapest option.

"Who do I pay every month?"

We are your 3rd party provider. You can setup auto pay with us, and cancel anytime.

"Can I use this for my home internet with an LTE router?"

This plan is advertised as a mobile phone plan. We do not offer any guarantee that this will work in a device that is not a mobile phone. Personal hotspot is also not guaranteed at full speed.

"Will my plan expire or price increase?"

Once you sign up your plan is locked in. No price increases will ever happen for an active line. Pricing is always increasing so order now to lock in your price!

"I have a dual sim phone, how does this work."

There is no way to port a number to our Bottomless Data Plan. To keep your preferred number we commonly suggest a secondary line. Newer iPhones and many other Androids have dual-sim capability. This allows 2 lines to be active simultaneously. The most popular combo is our plan + the $9 talk and text plan (Freedom Mobile)
