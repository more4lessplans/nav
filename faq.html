"What is the coverage of your plans like?"

Our Bottomless Data Plan gives you service in Canada, USA, and Mexico. We do not have any infrustructure of our own. We operate entirely off major carrier networks. The network and speeds are all top tier and unaltered. Depending on your plan, whether Rogers or Telus speeds may vary. Check the appropriate websites for speeds and coverage maps.

"I've seen people charging $600 or more to setup an unlimited data plan" 

There is no real market or competition so people can charge whatever they want. We beleive in competitive, fair pricing. We provide this service to help Canadians save money, while enjoying worry free benifits.

"I'm already with Rogers, am I still eligible?""

Yes. No matter what carrier you are with you can always change providers, or upgrade your current plan. Browse our plan selections or contact us for more info on your options.

"Can I keep my number?"

The short answer is yes! With our Rogers 20GB and 50GB plan, and our Telus plans we will transfer your number for you! Unlimited data is a little different but still possible. Contact us for more details.

"Can I get a new phone?""

We reccomend to everyone to bring their own device. This is actually the cheapest option.

"Who do I pay every month?"

It depends on your plan. You either pay Rogers or Telus directly, or us.

"Is this illegal?"

Not at all. These practices have been going on for years, and have made several news headlines.

"Can I use this for my home internet with an LTE router?"

We reccomend this plan for mobile phones only. That being said it is possible to get a router or hub working. We ask if you attempt to do this, not to abuse the unlimited data so it is still allowed.

"Will my plan expire or price increase?"

Once you sign up your plan is locked in. No price increases will ever happen for an active line. Pricing is always increasing so order now to lock in your price!
